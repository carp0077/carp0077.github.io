The Princess Bride

My favourite movie: The Princess Bride

The Princess Bride was inspired by a book of the same name written by William Goldman. In the movie, an elderly man reads the book "The Princess Bride" to his grandson, who has to stay home from school because he is sick. The book begins with the love story of Buttercup and Westley, and then follows them on an extensive adventure to reunite with one another when the Prince of the land tries to marry Buttercup.
